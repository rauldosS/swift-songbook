The Customization Bar is a section at the bottom of the chord page that offers various options to personalize the user experience. It allows you to adjust the font size, display chords, and access other features to make the chord view more suitable for each user's preferences.

The CHORDS shortcut allows you to quickly and conveniently view the chords of the songs. The chords will accompany you throughout the chord page.

The COLUMNS shortcut is a feature that allows users to view the chord of the song in column format. Enjoy a more compact and organized presentation of the chord, making the experience of playing the song even more enjoyable and intuitive. The control of the number of columns is in your hands!

The shortcut allows you to enlarge the chord view to occupy the entire screen of the device used, whether it's a computer, tablet, or smartphone.

The FONT shortcut allows users to adjust the text size of the chords according to their preferences. You can increase or decrease the font size to make the chord view more comfortable and suitable for your personal taste.
